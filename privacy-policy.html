Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Welcome! At My Contractions Tracker ("we", "us", or "our"), we value your privacy. This app helps pregnant women track contractions based on the 5-1-1 rule to determine when to go to the hospital.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the My Contractions Tracker mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Our app does not collect or store any personal data from users (as of 2026).

Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not track device IDs, IP addresses, or location data.
  • No user-input data (such as contraction timings) is stored on our servers or shared with anyone.

All tracking and calculations happen locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Ads to display banner advertisements in the app. Google Ads may collect certain information for ad personalization and analytics, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., type, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (e.g., views, clicks)
  • Anonymous identifiers for advertising purposes

This data is handled by Google and is subject to their privacy policies. We do not have access to this data.

For more details, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Data Practices

If you wish to opt out of personalized ads, you can do so through your device's settings (e.g., Google Settings > Ads on Android, or Limit Ad Tracking on iOS).

3. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
